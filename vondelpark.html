    Good to know
  • Barbecuing is not allowed.
  • Dogs can only roam freely in parts of the park

    • This beautiful park is free for anyone to enjoy. You can simply enter the park by it multiple entrances, and stroll trough it.

    Your paw friend can also enjoy the park. They do require a leash on most places, but in some designated area's they can run around freely.

    The park is the perfect place to run due the many paths. Don't fancy running? Just do some yoga on the grass or simply walk around.

    In and around the park are multible places where you can sit down and enjoy something to eat or drink.
